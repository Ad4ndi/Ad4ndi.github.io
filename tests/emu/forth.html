Forth stack emulator

Forth Commands:

Numbers: Any number can be pushed to the stack (e.g. 42, 7, -5).
Arithmetic: +, -, *, / - Perform operations on the stack.
Dup: Duplicate the top item on the stack.
Swap: Swap the top two items on the stack.
Drop: Drop the top item from the stack.
Clear: Clear the stack (use the word "clear").
